## Design System: Clay

Inspired by **Clay**: Organic, colorful gradients, bubbly shapes, creative agency energy

### Typography
Plus Jakarta Sans, 16px body, bold display 48-72px, generous line height

**Brand fonts (install via next/font or Google Fonts):**
- Display/Hero: `font-black` with tight letter-spacing
- Body: `font-normal` 14-16px, 1.6 line-height
- Mono (numbers/code): `font-mono`

### Color Palette
Rich gradients: purple→pink→orange, white text, translucent cards

**Brand colors:**
- Primary 1: `#ef4444`
- Primary 2: `#dc2626`
- Primary 3: `#1f1f1f`

### Borders & Radius
Very generous: 24-32px cards, 999px pills, organic shapes

### Shadows
Colorful glows matching brand, 0 20px 60px rgba(color,0.3)

### Spacing Scale
8/16/24/40/80px generous, breathing room between sections

### Design Principles
- Color as personality — bold and intentional
- Organic shapes break grid monotony
- Motion is part of the brand
- Every section has a unique visual treatment
